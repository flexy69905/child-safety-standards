Swiply Child Safety Policy

1. Commitment to Child Safety

Swiply is committed to providing a safe and secure environment for users. We strictly prohibit child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and have implemented measures to detect, prevent, and report such activities.

2. Policies Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

  • Swiply is only for users aged 18 and above.
  • We use AI-based flagging and human moderation to detect inappropriate content.
  • Users can report suspicious behavior, and we take immediate action.
  • Any offender will be permanently banned and reported to authorities.

3. In-App User Reporting Mechanism

Users can report profiles, messages, posts, or comments violating community guidelines.

Our moderation team reviews reports within 24 hours and takes necessary action.

4. Preventing & Addressing Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)

  • Automatic Content Moderation: AI detects and flags violations.
  • Manual Moderation: Reported content is reviewed and removed immediately.
  • Legal Reporting: We report CSAM to law enforcement agencies.
  • Permanent Bans: Users involved in CSAM are permanently banned.

5. Compliance with Global Child Safety Laws

Swiply complies with international laws, including:

  • COPPA (USA)
  • GDPR-K (Europe)
  • UAE Child Protection Laws

6. Designated Child Safety Contact

For concerns, please contact our Child Safety Officer:

Email: childsafety@swiplyapp.com

7. User Education & Awareness

We provide guides on online safety, reminders to report issues, and resources for safer interactions.
